Data Analyst Guide: Matching Criteria, Signals, and Advice

Purpose
This guide supports retrieval for Data Analyst, BI Analyst, and Product Analyst roles.

Core Skills Recruiters Expect
Data extraction and transformation:
SQL
joins
aggregations
CTEs
window functions
data cleaning

Analysis tooling:
Python or R
pandas
Jupyter notebooks
spreadsheets

Visualization and communication:
Tableau
Power BI
Looker or similar BI tools
dashboard design
storytelling with data

Analytical foundations:
statistics
hypothesis testing
A/B testing
metric design
trend analysis

Business alignment:
KPI understanding
stakeholder communication
requirement clarification
interpretation of results

Signals of a Strong Candidate
Can translate a business question into a measurable analysis plan.
Can write robust SQL and explain metric definitions.
Built dashboards used by real stakeholders.
Understands limitations, assumptions, and data quality issues.
Shows business impact, not just tool usage.

Important Resume Evidence
Dashboards built and who used them.
Automation or reporting improvements.
Experimentation or statistical analysis.
Data volume or process complexity.
Business decisions influenced by the analysis.

Common Gaps
Tool list without analytical outcomes.
Dashboard experience without SQL depth.
Python listed but not used for analysis.
No evidence of stakeholder communication.
No understanding of metrics, experimentation, or limitations.

Advice by Seniority
Junior:
Show SQL projects, one dashboard, one end-to-end analysis, and clear metric reasoning.

Middle:
Show ownership of recurring reporting, decision support, experimentation, and stakeholder communication.

Senior:
Show analytical leadership, KPI design, prioritization, influence, and process standardization.

What to Emphasize in Matching Explanations
Matched_skills should prioritize SQL, dashboarding, Python, statistics, and business impact.
Missing_skills should prioritize SQL depth and business reasoning before niche tools.
Strong explanations should mention both analysis quality and communication value.
