Marketing Guide: Matching Criteria, Signals, and Advice

Purpose
This guide supports retrieval for digital marketing, growth marketing, performance marketing, and general marketing specialist roles.

Core Skills Recruiters Expect
Channel and campaign execution:
digital marketing
campaign management
email marketing
social media marketing
paid acquisition
content strategy

Performance tooling:
Google Analytics
Google Ads
Meta Ads
CRM systems
marketing automation tools such as HubSpot

Optimization and measurement:
funnel analysis
conversion rate optimization
A/B testing
audience segmentation
reporting and attribution basics

Content and communication:
copywriting
landing page collaboration
creative briefing
stakeholder coordination

Signals of a Strong Candidate
Can connect activities to business outcomes such as leads, pipeline, CAC, ROAS, conversion rate, or retention.
Can explain why a channel or tactic was selected.
Can interpret campaign data and make optimization decisions.
Can coordinate across content, design, sales, and product teams.
Shows both strategic thinking and hands-on execution.

Important Resume Evidence
Budget sizes or campaign scale.
Lead generation or conversion improvements.
Channel mix used.
Reporting cadence and business stakeholders supported.
Examples of experiments and optimization cycles.

Common Gaps
Marketing tools listed without business impact.
Content experience without analytics depth.
Paid acquisition experience without attribution understanding.
Good execution but no prioritization logic.
No evidence of funnel thinking or experimentation.

Advice by Seniority
Junior:
Show campaign support tasks, analytics basics, content production, and one metrics-driven case study.

Middle:
Show channel ownership, reporting, optimization loops, and measurable growth outcomes.

Senior:
Show strategy, budget decisions, cross-functional leadership, and business impact over time.

What to Emphasize in Matching Explanations
Matched_skills should prioritize channel execution, analytics, campaign optimization, and business metrics.
Missing_skills should prioritize measurable performance and channel depth before cosmetic marketing tools.
Strong explanations should mention both execution quality and growth impact.
